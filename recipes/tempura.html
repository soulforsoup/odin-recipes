How to Make Hot Hot Tempura!

Tempura is a popular Japanese deep-fried seafood/vegetables encased in a light yet crisp batter. Learn how to make perfect tempura at home!

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Vehicle Oil

Let's begin!

  1. Mash that flour and water together.
  2. Just kinda hope it becomes doughy.
  3. Wrap that tempura in that congealed flour/water combo.
  4. Heat that oil. If it starts screaming, lower the fire.
  5. Throw that prawn and flour and water combo in.
  6. Fry until golden brown.
