Making Slurptastic Udon

Beef udon is a Japanese comfort dish made of tender sliced beef seasoned and stir fried on top of warm udon noodle in a savory dashi broth.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Kombu
  • Dashi
  • Ice

Let's begin!

  1. Mash the water and flour together, then let it rest.
  2. Roll the dough into long straw-like shapes. We call this noodles.
  3. Boil the water in a pot, and kinda just add kombu and dashi and cook it real long.
  4. Yum, that soup smells great. Now throw the noodles in.
  5. Using the power of lucky guesses, estimate the amount of cooking time based on your star sign.
  6. If it's done, it's done. Now eat.
